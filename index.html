Physicals

HealthSynch has partnered with facilities that can offer you any type of physical.

Pre-Employment Physicals - Our network of clinic partners offer many types of pre-employment physicals to assess the applicant's overall health and fitness for the job requirements.

DOT Physicals - Mandatory for individuals holding or applying for a commercial driver's license, ensuring they meet medical standards for safely operating commercial vehicles.

Respiratory Physicals - Required by OSHA for employees who use a respirator, evaluated by a physician or other licensed health care professional.

Fitness for Duty Exams - Establishes whether workers are physically, mentally, and emotionally well enough for duty. Tests are tailored to the employer's requirements and can target different aspects of the employee's condition.

Return to Work Physicals - Required after an employee has been absent due to illness or injury, ensuring they are fit to resume their duties safely.
